Let It Bee Apiary

Mike's honey from Let It Bee Apiary is always natural, unfiltered, and local. He believes in delivering the best product to his customers.

Mike's Tips on Starting Your Own Hive

  • Know where you are getting your bees from
  • Do your research before
  • Get ready to work hard to take care of your bees
  • Join a local bee club, either in Eau Claire or Chippewa Falls
  • Locate the bees in an area where they will be able to easily collect pollen
  • Make sure you have the right equipment
  • Have fun with it!

Our Story

Let Bee Apiary is located in Dunn County, WI. Mike has owned bees for many years now and has sold hundreds of pounds of honey. Each sprig he gets a new set of bees which he keeps on his property. He goes to farers markets, vendor markets, and more to sell his honey.
